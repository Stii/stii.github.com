It's not a duck

Posted: Tue, 14 Aug 2012
  • blogging

I’ve been gone for a while. Off the radar. Enjoying my free time. Spending as much of it as possible longboarding and chilling with family and friends.

After we shut down Afrigator I realised: Blogging is dead. Like a zombie. Let’s face it, there is better things to do than reproducing news. It simply is not a priority any more. All the while, I’ve been missing it. Writing is fun. It makes you think. It is an outlet. I still have lots on my mind. Life is full of lessons, why not teach others what you’ve learned?

Which brings me to my point. While it looks like a blog, acts like a blog, it’s not a blog. It is merely a site. My site where I can do what I want, when I want.
